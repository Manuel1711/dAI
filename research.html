Research Hub

Working notes and analytical directions for ERC-8004 agent ecosystems.

What is ERC-8004?

ERC-8004 standardizes identity + feedback registries for autonomous AI agents. It enables on-chain discoverability, portable reputation and transparent ownership transitions.

Research Directions

  • Network structure: agent-client bipartite topology
  • Reputation dynamics: score stability and volatility
  • Behavioral patterns: retention and feedback cadence

Method

Data are built from canonical on-chain events (Registered, Transfer, NewFeedback, FeedbackRevoked) with periodic refresh and checkpointed indexing.

Current Caveats

  • Category metadata still partially unresolved
  • Workflow reliability under public RPC limits
  • Reorg-hardening to be extended
